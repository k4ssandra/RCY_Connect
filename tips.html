First Aid & Wellness Tips

Educational resources to keep you safe and healthy. Learn essential first aid techniques and wellness practices.

Health Safety Emergency Response

CPR Basics

Learn the fundamental steps of cardiopulmonary resuscitation to save lives in emergency situations.

  • Check for responsiveness and breathing
  • Call emergency services immediately
  • Perform 30 chest compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths
  • Continue until help arrives or the person shows signs of life
Health

Fire Safety

Essential tips for preventing fires and what to do if one occurs in your home or community.

  • Install and test smoke alarms regularly
  • Keep fire extinguishers accessible and know how to use them
  • Have an escape plan and practice it with family
  • If fire occurs, get out, stay out, and call for help
Safety

Choking Response

Quick actions to take when someone is choking, including the Heimlich maneuver technique.

  • Ask if they can speak or cough
  • Perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver) if conscious
  • For unconscious person, start CPR
  • Call emergency services immediately
Emergency Response

Wound Care

Proper cleaning and dressing of cuts and wounds to prevent infection and promote healing.

  • Stop bleeding by applying pressure
  • Clean with soap and water
  • Apply antibiotic ointment and cover with bandage
  • Change dressing daily and watch for infection signs
Health

Heat Stroke Prevention

Recognize signs of heat exhaustion and stroke, and learn prevention strategies for hot weather.

  • Stay hydrated and avoid excessive sun exposure
  • Wear light clothing and use sunscreen
  • Take breaks in shade during outdoor activities
  • Recognize symptoms: dizziness, nausea, rapid heartbeat

Mental Health Support

Understanding mental health challenges and knowing when and how to seek help for yourself or others.

  • Recognize signs of distress in yourself and others
  • Listen without judgment and offer support
  • Encourage professional help when needed
  • Practice self-care and maintain healthy routines
