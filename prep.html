Emergency Hotlines

Critical contact numbers for immediate assistance during emergencies. Keep these numbers saved and accessible.

  • National Emergency Hotline: 911
  • Red Cross Emergency: 1-800-RED-CROSS
  • Disaster Response: 1-800-HELP-NOW
  • Local Fire Department: Contact your local authorities

Disaster Kit Checklist

Essential items to include in your emergency preparedness kit. Review and assemble your kit today.

  • Non-perishable food (3-day supply)
  • Water (1 gallon per person per day)
  • First aid kit and medications
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Important documents and cash
  • Blankets and warm clothing
